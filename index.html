üöó Auto Insurance Claims Process

Follow these steps when filing an auto insurance claim in North Carolina

1

Ensure Safety

Check for injuries and call 911 if needed. Move to a safe location if possible.

2

Call Police

Report the accident to police even for minor crashes. Get the report number.

3

Exchange Information

Get driver info, insurance details, and witness contact information.

4

Document Scene

Take photos of vehicles, damage, scene, and write down what happened.

5

Contact Insurance

Call your insurance company to report the claim as soon as possible.

6

Work with Adjuster

Schedule inspection and work with adjuster to assess damage and coverage.

üìã NC Minimum Insurance Requirements

North Carolina requires minimum coverage of 50/100/50 (Bodily Injury per person/per accident, Property Damage)

üè† Home Insurance Claims Process

Step-by-step guide for filing home insurance claims in North Carolina

1

Ensure Safety

Make sure everyone is safe. Don't enter damaged areas until cleared by officials.

2

Document Damage

Take photos and videos of all damage before making any temporary repairs.

3

Contact Insurance

Report the claim to your insurance company immediately.

4

Mitigate Damage

Make reasonable temporary repairs to prevent further damage. Save receipts.
